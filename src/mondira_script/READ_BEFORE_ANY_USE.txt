Mondira Script is a contemporary calligraphy typeface with a refined, flowing rhythm and a calm sense of balance.

The letters move with a smooth, deliberate pace, shaped by gentle contrasts and long, confident strokes. Its curves feel soft rather than ornate, and the overall structure stays clear even when the writing becomes expressive. Mondira Script has an elegant presence without leaning on excess decoration, making it feel natural and composed.

The style suggests handwritten notes prepared with care, formal yet personal, where each word is allowed to breathe. It works comfortably in longer phrases, keeping a consistent tone while subtle variations add life and warmth.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed with attention to flow and detail, Mondira Script reflects a modern approach to calligraphy where readability and expression are given equal importance. It is meant to feel written by hand, not performed, with a steady confidence that comes from restraint rather than flourish.
